# When we get a problem to be solved, where should we start?

- Understand the problem.
  - Find who is the participant in the process facing the problem
  - Find out what is process the problem resides (Problem domain)
  - Find out the minimum process that the problem process resides (Context)
  - Find out the participants within above process
  - Find out the collaborations from above processes
  - Find out the entity relations from above collaborations
